# PolicyCenter User Guide
## Cross-Product: Renewal While Claim Is Open
### Detail 1
When PolicyCenter processes a renewal for a policy with an open claim in ClaimCenter, the renewal itself does not alter the terms applied to the existing claim. The new term's coverage and deductible apply only to losses occurring on or after the new effective date. This is the same rule referenced in ClaimCenter's 'Renewal Impact on Open Claims' section, and is the most frequent cross-product question raised by claims adjusters during renewal season.
