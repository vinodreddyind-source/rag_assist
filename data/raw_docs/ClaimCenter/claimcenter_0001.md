# ClaimCenter User Guide
## First Notice of Loss (FNOL) Intake
### Detail 1
When an FNOL is submitted through ClaimContactScreen, the adjuster must confirm the loss date, policy number, and line of business (LOB) before the claim can be created in ClaimCenter. For auto claims involving bodily injury (BI), the system requires an additional injury-details step before the claim can move to the Coverage Review stage.

### Detail 2
FNOL calls routed through the SIU (special investigations unit) flag are held for manual review if the reported loss amount exceeds $50,000 or if the same policy number has more than two prior FNOL entries in 12 months.
