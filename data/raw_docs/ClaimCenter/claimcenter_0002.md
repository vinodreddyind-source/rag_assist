# ClaimCenter User Guide
## Deductible Calculation
### Detail 1
The deductible applied to a claim is read from the policy's effective coverage terms at the loss date, not the terms in effect when the claim is processed. This distinction matters most when a policy renewal occurs between the loss date and the claim processing date -- the pre-renewal deductible still applies to a loss that occurred before renewal.

### Detail 2
For property damage (PD) claims with multiple covered items, ClaimCenter applies a single per-occurrence deductible rather than a per-item deductible, unless the policy's PolicyCenter terms explicitly define split deductibles for that line of business.
